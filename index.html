Features

Entangled liquidity pools

Provide any token as liquidity to any pool on DEXFAI. No more token pairs, no more fragmented liquidity. Through DEXFAI’s entangled pools, deep liquidity becomes available to everyone.

Swap assets

Trade your assets with any other asset. Through DEXFAI’s bridge token mechanics, every asset becomes automatically swappable with every other asset on DEXFAI, without having to experience high slippage.

Liquid NFT

Stake XFIT to generate Liquid NFTs, a new type of NFT with a deterministic underlying value. Holders of Liquid NFTs receive rewards from DEXFAI’s continuous burning mechanism.

Farming events

Have a new idea for a project? Create your own token and / or farming event on DEXFAI with only a few clicks. Through farming events on DEXFAI, startups and organizations can bootstrap their own community driven market making.

Manage liquidity

View and manage all your investments in one place. DEXFAI’s asset management dashboard allows liquidity providers to manage all assets stress free.

Earn rewards

Accumulate rewards by providing liquidity to pools and farming events. Through DEXFAI’s entangled liquidity pools, providing liquidity to tokens and projects that you believe in has never been easier.
